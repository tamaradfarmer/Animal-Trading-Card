Axolotl

An Axolotl can regenerate body parts

  • Scientific Name: Ambystoma mexicanum
  • Average Length : 12 inches
  • Average Lifespan : 10-15 years
  • Habitat : Lake Xochimilco in the Valley of Mexico

These pale smiling amphibians are native only to Mexico City. They are rumoured to be named after the Aztec god Xolotl, who disguised himself as a salamander to avoid being sacrificed.They can regenerate lost limbs to stay young. Axolotls are currenltly critically endangered
